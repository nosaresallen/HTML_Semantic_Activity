What is MaestroMist.?

MaestroMist was founded last July 2023. MaestroMist is a mini vape shop and focusing on selling a disposable e-cigarette products.

What is Disposable Vape?

Disposable vape devices are as the name suggests. They come ready to use, prefilled with e-liquid, with a battery and can be used straight away. Once the e-liquid or the battery runs out they cannot be recharged or refilled. So then need to be disposed of.
